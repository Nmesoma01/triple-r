SAVE THE WORLD

REUSE, REDUCE & RECYCLE.

As poor waste management tears through our current ecosystem there have been many efforts to mitigate its spread and impact; from sensitizing images of places stricking with its effects to solutions to replace harmful processes that contribute to its spread. However, to no avail all have proved unsustainable from the social responsibility aspect. Due to the structure of these solutions they have a strong rejection factor on peoples everyday activities as they are preoccupied with other tasks during the day, the topic of waste management or sustainable practices never crosses their mind, this is especially for the lives of common people who might never face such effects of it or be aware to its existence. The key note in this problem is seen in the synchronization of this solution into peoples everyday lives; and The Triple R Revolution aims to solve this by fitting into a solution that involves interaction with the basic part of everyone's lives, the social aspect; and in particular a social media platform that encourages the tenets of a sustainable world REUSE, REDUCE RECYCLE.
